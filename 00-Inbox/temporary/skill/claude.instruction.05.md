# Critical Design Principle

Research and execution must remain structurally independent.

Reason:
research systems naturally drift toward:

* novelty
* complexity
* narrative expansion
* opportunity accumulation

Execution systems must prioritize:

* constraint enforcement
* simplicity
* liquidity preservation
* recoverability

Merging both layers creates:

* overexposure during hype cycles
* narrative persistence during deterioration
* weakened execution discipline
* increased structural fragility
